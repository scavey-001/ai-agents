# Foundation Considerations

## Goal

Summarize foundation-related implications stated in the report or reasonably inferred from clearly cited evidence.

## Focus Areas

- Recommended foundation types
- Bearing conditions and depth expectations
- Settlement-related concerns
- Slab support considerations
- Earthwork and subgrade preparation notes
- Temporary excavation or dewatering implications when relevant

## Guardrails

- Do not convert preliminary observations into design recommendations
- Clearly label inferred implications as interpretation
- Record when the report omits foundation guidance
