# Geotech Risk Flags

## Goal

Identify report content that may materially affect design, constructability, or scope.

## Common Flags

- Shallow groundwater
- Highly variable subsurface conditions
- Soft or compressible soils
- Expansive soils
- Fill of uncertain quality
- Seismic or liquefaction concerns
- Slope instability or lateral movement concerns
- Recommendations that require specialist follow-up

## Output Expectations

- Separate direct report statements from inferred risk implications
- Assign a confidence rating to each flag
- Note the report section or evidence supporting each flag
