# Boring Log Extraction

## Goal

Extract boring identifiers, depths, groundwater observations, and notable subsurface transitions from report text or logs.

## Focus Areas

- Boring or sounding IDs
- Surface elevation and termination depth
- Groundwater depth and observation timing
- Soil and rock strata changes
- Sampling intervals and recovery notes when available

## Output Expectations

- Maintain original units
- Keep each boring traceable to its source page or section
- Mark uncertain readings with a confidence rating
